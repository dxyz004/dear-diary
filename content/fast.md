**FAST** is a one-number metric to help inform of the next two-way<sup>1</sup> NBA stars.

**FAST** stands for:
- **F**ree throws
- **A**ge
- **S**teals
- **T**urnovers

| ----------- | ----------- |
| Jabari Smith      | 100       |
| Paolo Banchero   | 99        |

---

<sup>1</sup> - Weighted combined offensive and defensive impact. E.g., **FAST** likes Chris Paul more than Trae Young.
